How To Use

Random picker is an online tool which helps to choose someone or the winners, making a group based on entries, and to mix list of things.

How to use?
  1. Input / copy your list from your word, wps, and TXT file.
  2. Paste the list to the form box
  3. Choose the random mixer that you like beside of form box.
